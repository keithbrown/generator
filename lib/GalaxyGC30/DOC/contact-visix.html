Your feedback is important to us! Please help us make Galaxy a better application development environment for you.

To report bugs or request technical assistance:

  • Fill in the Submit Galaxy Report form on Visix's web site.

    or...

  • Use the Contact Visix menu item (available from any Help menu in the Visual Resource Builder) to access the Submit Problem Report form.

    Using the Web page above helps us to answer any questions you have or find solutions to a problem most effectively, and is the preferred method. If, however, you do not have web access, you can email us:

  • For technical assistance, send email to galaxy-support@visix.com. To report a bug, send email to galaxy-bugs@visix.com

You can also send your suggestions, comments, compliments or complaints via email to galaxy-feedback@visix.com
